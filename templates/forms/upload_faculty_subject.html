Important: Assigning a subject to a batch (via Batch-Subject CSV) does not automatically assign a faculty member. You must separately upload faculty-subject mappings here so the timetable solver knows who teaches what. Until a faculty is assigned to a subject for a batch, that subject will be skipped during timetable generation.

CSV Format

Required columns (all three must be present):

  • faculty_emp_id — Employee ID of the faculty
  • subject_code — Subject code
  • batch_section — Section name of the batch (e.g. 2A(AIML))
